Loan Shark is a simple and intuitive tracking tool designed to help schools and organisations manage their IT device loans. Whether it’s laptops, tablets, or other equipment, our goal is to make tracking easy, efficient, and accessible.

This project started as a simple idea — to make “who has what” easier to manage without complicated software. Built with modern web standards, Loan Shark is lightweight, fast, and made to grow with your needs.
